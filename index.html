OUR STORY

Pizzon is not your average pizzeria.
Founded in 1988, Pizzon combines old world quality,
values and work ethic with new world ambiance and award winning appeal.
Today, we continues to make fresh dough daily — often twice daily — from scratch.
Produce is delivered 6 days a week to ensure only the freshest ingredients possible.
We only use one hundred percent whole milk mozzarella grated daily on site and
nothing but fresh toppings, as we know there is no substitute for quality.
While quality, handmade pizza created in the classic New York tradition
is the cornerstone of Pizzon,the ambiance of the restaurant adds to its appeal.
